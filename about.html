How to Make Risotto

  1. simmer 7 cups good chicken stock, keep on backburner
  2. sweat 1/2 to 1 C diced onions in butter
  3. add 2 cups of arborio, toast in pan 2 mins
  4. add 1/2 C white wine, cook down 1 min
  5. add stock by the ladleful, allowing rice to absorb the liquid before adding more (it should take a while, don't rush it)
  6. when rice reaches desired doneness, stir in grated Parmigiano Reggiano and more butter
